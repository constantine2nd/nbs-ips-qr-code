What is NBS IPS QR?

NBS IPS QR codes enable instant payments in Serbia through the National Bank's Instant Payment System (IPS). Generate valid QR codes for invoices, bills, and payment requests that comply with Serbian banking standards.

Important Notice

For Payment Display: When using generated NBS IPS QR codes, you must display the text "NBS IPS QR" next to, below, or above the QR code on invoices, bills, or payment requests.

For Merchant Payments: IPS QR codes with PT and EK identifiers for point-of-sale payments require a contractual relationship with an acquiring bank.

Service: The NBS IPS QR Generator/Validator API service is provided free of charge by the National Bank of Serbia.
